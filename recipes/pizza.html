Pizza Recipe

Description

The authentic neapolitan pizza, the Margarita. This pizza features tomato sauce, mozzarella, and basil, with just a hint of sea salt.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Sauce
  • 20 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 20 leaves fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Sea salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven with a pizza stone to the max temperature possible (ideally over 300ยบ degrees C).
  2. Stretch out and pat 1 dough ball to form a circle 10 to 12 inches in diameter. Place dough on a lightly floured pizza peel.
  3. Top with 1/2 cup of tomato sauce and spread to cover within an inch of the edge of the dough.
  4. Arrange 5 slices of mozzarella cheese on top of the tomato sauce, then place 5 basil leaves on top.
  5. Drizzle pizza with 1 tablespoon olive oil and sprinkle with sea salt to taste.
  6. Slide each pizza onto the pizza stone in the preheated oven. Bake until cheese is bubbly and the underside of the crust is golden brown, 5 to 7 minutes.
